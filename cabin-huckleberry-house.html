About This Cabin

The Huckleberry House is a spacious family-friendly retreat that can accommodate up to four guests. With two bedrooms and a fully equipped kitchen, it's perfect for couples traveling together or small families.

The second bedroom can be opened upon request for additional guests. Contact us for rates on additional occupancy. Enjoy the large living area, cozy fireplace, and private deck with mountain views.
