Getting Here

The Cabins at Country Road is located in Evergreen, Colorado, approximately 30 minutes west of Denver on the historic Lariat Loop.

From Denver

Take I-70 West to Exit 252 (Evergreen Parkway). Head south on Evergreen Parkway (Highway 74) for approximately 8 miles. We're located just past the Country Road Cafe.

From Golden

Take the Lariat Loop (Highway 40) up Lookout Mountain, then continue on Highway 74 through Kittredge to Evergreen. Enjoy the scenic drive!

Before You Arrive

A few things to know for your journey

Mountain Roads

The drive involves winding mountain roads. Take your time and enjoy the scenery.

Winter Driving

In winter months, 4WD or AWD is recommended. Check road conditions before traveling.

Cell Service

Cell coverage is spotty in the mountains. Download directions before you leave.
