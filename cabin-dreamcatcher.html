About This Cabin

This cabin will captivate you with its cozy design and breath-taking creek views!

Decorated with authentic Indian art throughout — the stucco walls are designed to complement the light hickory wood floors and cathedral pine ceilings. All of which add to the romantic feel of this creekside cabin.

Enjoy the comfortable furnishings and a 4-poster lodgepole king-size bed that awaits your peaceful dreams. You will love the rustic stone bathroom design that features an oversized shower and a petrified wood sink.

This cabin also features a full kitchen. After spending a few nights at the The Dreamcatcher, you will leave feeling completely relaxed and rejuvenated.

This is an adults-only cabin.
