About This Cabin

The Bootlegger Barn is a unique historic barn conversion that offers an unforgettable stay. With its soaring ceilings and authentic barn architecture, this cabin transports you to another era while providing all modern comforts.

Please note this cabin features a kitchenette with refrigerator and microwave rather than a full kitchen - perfect for light meals and snacks. The nearby Country Road Cafe offers excellent dining options just steps away.
