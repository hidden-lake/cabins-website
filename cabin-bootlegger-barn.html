About This Cabin

Imagine the time of Prohibition when our Colorado Outlaws were concocting their moonshine in this very barn. As we lovingly restored this old barn, we unearthed various bottles, and with this, a great cabin was born.

Enter the Bootlegger's Barn, a rustic yet elegant retreat bringing you back to another era in time. Imagine a wall of whisky barrels full of cascading fresh mountain water for your shower. Reclaimed wood floors. Lead pane stained glass windows. An open bedroom upstairs loft with a queen size feather bed with amazing views of Bear Creek.

Amenities include a flat-screen TV, wood deck, gas fireplace, and no cooking facilities.

This is an adults-only cabin.
