About This Cabin

The Chicken Coop offers rustic-themed lodging with charming country character. Don't let the name fool you - this cozy cabin has been beautifully renovated while preserving its unique heritage and character.

With a fully equipped kitchen, comfortable bedroom with king bed, and a warm fireplace, this cabin offers the perfect blend of rustic charm and modern comfort.
