About This Cabin

The Fish Camp is a charming streamside retreat perfect for couples seeking a peaceful getaway. Located right along Bear Creek, you'll fall asleep to the soothing sounds of the water and wake up to stunning mountain views.

This cozy one-bedroom cabin features a fully equipped kitchen, comfortable living area with a gas fireplace, and all the amenities you need for a relaxing stay in the mountains.
