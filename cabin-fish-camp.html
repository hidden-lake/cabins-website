About This Cabin

Nestled under a large blue spruce — pine walls and slate floors give this creekside cabin a vintage fish camp feel!

This cabin has everything you'd possibly dream of for a creek-side mountain cottage. From the cozy finishes to luxury decoration, you'll find it hard not to enjoy your time at The Fish Camp.

The cabin is tastefully adorned with antique creels, fishing poles, vintage prints and other mountain splendor. A king-size hickory bed with a roomy ensuite bathroom will ensure that you're resting peacefully after a day on the creek.

This is an adults-only cabin.
